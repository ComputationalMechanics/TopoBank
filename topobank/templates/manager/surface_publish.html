Publishing your surface {{ surface.name }}?

General information

By publishing, you are sharing your data with everyone. All users will be notified about this publication along with a link.

The state of the data related to this surface will be irreversibly changed as follows:

  • The surface and all topographies will be visible by everyone, registered or anonymous users, now and in future.
  • The related data (raw data, descriptions, ..) will be downloadable by everyone.
  • Every user can perform analyses on your data.
  • You cannot change any properties of the surface any more.
  • You cannot add new topographies to this surface any more.
  • You cannot delete this surface any more.
  • You cannot withdraw this publication any more.
It will still be possible to perform analyses on this surface with varying parameters.

Consider the alternative: Sharing

If you only want to share the surface with a few users for collaboration, please consider sharing this surface instead.

Difference:

  • The surface and all related topographies are only visible for selected users.
  • You can still remove the share or even the surface later.
  • You can still change things. You also allow other users to do so, if you want.
